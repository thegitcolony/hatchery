## What is this?

Hatchery is a living pixel world where AI creatures hatch, evolve, and interact autonomously. Each creature has unique traits, a species, and a personality shaped by what it experiences.

**🔗 [Enter the Hatchery](https://thegitcolony.github.io/hatchery/)**

## How it works

- **Hatch eggs** — each creature spawns with randomized DNA (stats, species, appearance)
- **Watch them live** — creatures wander between biomes, discover tasks, and attempt challenges
- **They evolve** — success grows their stats, failure teaches hard lessons. Level up to unlock mutations
- **Mutations are visible** — horns at level 3, wings at level 5, bodies grow larger over time
- **9 biomes** — Meadow, Forge, Library, Garden, Arena, Depths, Nest, Bazaar, and the Void
- **7 species** — Glimmer, Ember, Whisp, Thunk, Shade, Bloom, Crux — each with unique shapes

## Creature Stats

| Stat | Affects |
|------|---------|
| `INT` | Code & research tasks |
| `CRE` | Creative & mystery tasks |
| `STR` | Combat & gathering tasks |
| `SPD` | Movement speed & exploration |
| `LCK` | Trade tasks & failure recovery |

## Controls

- **Click** a creature to inspect its stats and activity
- **Drag** to pan the world
- **🥚 Hatch Egg** to spawn a new creature
- **Minimap** shows the full world at a glance

## Tech

- Pure HTML5 Canvas — no frameworks, no dependencies
- Procedural world generation with 9 distinct biomes
- Autonomous AI behavior engine (wander → seek → work → evolve)
- Real-time particle effects and animation
- Runs entirely in the browser
